ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
===================

Visual insights into productivity patterns and time allocation.

Overview
--------
The Analytics page provides comprehensive data visualization of work patterns, helping users understand their productivity trends and make informed decisions.

Time Period Selection
---------------------
- Week view (default)
- Month view
- Quarter view
- Comparison mode (vs previous period)

Chart Types
-----------

1. HOURS CHART
   - Daily hours worked
   - Line or bar visualization
   - Shows pattern over time
   - Identifies high/low days

2. PRODUCTIVITY CHART
   - Productivity score per day
   - Based on hours vs target
   - Highlights optimal days
   - Trend line overlay

3. PROJECT BREAKDOWN
   - Pie/donut chart
   - Hours per project
   - Color-coded by project
   - Percentage distribution

4. HOURLY DISTRIBUTION
   - Heat map of working hours
   - Shows peak productivity times
   - 24-hour breakdown
   - Useful for schedule optimization

Key Metrics
-----------
- Total hours (period)
- Average hours per day
- Most productive day
- Least productive day
- Peak working hour
- Project with most time
- Consistency score

Insights Engine
---------------
The system generates automatic insights:

POSITIVE INSIGHTS
- "You worked 15% more than last week!"
- "Your consistency improved this month."
- "You hit your daily goal 5 days in a row!"

NEUTRAL INSIGHTS
- "Your peak hours are between 9 AM - 11 AM."
- "You work most on Wednesdays."
- "Client A gets 40% of your time."

WARNING INSIGHTS
- "You worked 20% less than last week."
- "Your break time decreased - remember to rest!"
- "Weekend work detected - maintain work-life balance."

Data Sources
------------
- time_entries table
- Calculated on-demand
- Cached for performance
- Real-time for current day

Export Options
--------------
- PDF report with charts
- CSV data export
- Date range selection
- Branded output
