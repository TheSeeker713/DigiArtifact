GOAL TRACKING SYSTEM
====================

Set, track, and achieve personal and professional milestones.

Overview
--------
The Goals feature helps users define targets for their work hours and track progress towards them with visual feedback and gamification elements.

Goal Types
----------

1. TOTAL HOURS GOALS
   - Track overall hours worked
   - Period-based (daily, weekly, monthly)
   - Not tied to specific project
   - Example: "Work 40 hours this week"

2. PROJECT GOALS
   - Track hours on specific project
   - Period-based
   - Linked to project entity
   - Example: "Spend 15 hours on Client A this week"

Goal Periods
------------
- Daily: Resets every day at midnight
- Weekly: Monday through Sunday
- Monthly: First to last day of month
- Custom: User-defined date range

- Daily target

WORK-LIFE BALANCE
- 35 hours/week
- Sustainable pace
- Icon: Balance scale

Progress Tracking
-----------------
- Real-time progress bar
- Percentage complete
- Hours remaining
- On-track indicator
- Ahead/behind status

Streak System
-------------
- Consecutive goal completions
- Streak counter per goal
- Record streak tracking
- Streak protection (grace period)

Visual States
-------------
- Not started: Gray
- In progress: Gold (< 80%)
- Almost there: Green (>= 80%)
- Complete: Bright green + checkmark
- Overtime: Purple glow

Gamification
------------
- XP awarded for goal completion
- Bonus XP for streak maintenance
- Achievement badges:
  - First goal completed
  - 7-day streak
  - 30-day streak
  - 100 goals completed

Statistics Dashboard
--------------------
- Active goals count
- Completed goals count
- Average progress percentage
- Longest streak
- Completion rate
